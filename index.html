Welcome

Zijian Zhang received the B.E. degree in electronic engineering from Tsinghua University (THU), Beijing, China, in 2020. He is currently working toward the Ph.D. degree in electronic engineering from THU, under the guidance of Prof. Linglong Dai. His research interests include physical-layer signal processing for massive MIMO and reconfigurable intelligent surfaces (RIS). He has received the National Scholarship in 2019 and the Excellent Undergraduate Thesis Award in 2020.
